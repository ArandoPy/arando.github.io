The AranDo Project

The AranDo Project is an open door to the English language. For the moment, the project focuses on elementary students. And most importantly, it focuses on granting Paraguayan public schools a stable resource to teach English. Lesson plans, materials, games, and many other tools will be found in the platform. Ready and easy to use!

Teachers Role

Teachers are valuable members of this ecosystem. Their role is to help the student through their discoveries. And most importantly, their role isto be aware and actively seek progress.

  • Always connected, in and out of the classroom
  • Focused on actual progress and not only grades
  • Their goal is to be a facilitator not a language professional
  • Their time and input are considered and important

Students Role

Our goal is for students to learn the language through exploration and real content. Their role is to be constantly connected to the classroom. Also, our materials are as student-friendly as possible so they can enjoy their adventures.

  • Always connected, in and out of the classroom
  • Learning through meaningful content
  • Active and cooperative learners, no boredom here
  • Quizzes, games, you name it. ItÂ´s here

Tutors Role

At AranDo, we acknowledge the bigger picture. The child is not isolated to the classroom. That is why parents and home time are key to their progress. Therefore, we have included the parents as a fundamental pillar of education.

  • Active members of the learning process
  • They should aid and promote a broader learning experience
  • Their input is valued
